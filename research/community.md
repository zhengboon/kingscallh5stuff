# Community: Kings and Legends / Rise of Mythos / King's Call

## Overview

The community for this game series is fragmented across multiple platforms, eras, and regional versions. The overall community size has declined significantly since the game's peak during the Rise of Mythos era (2013–2019). Kings Call Steam player counts in 2024 peaked at approximately 508 concurrent players (August 2024). Community consensus describes it as a niche, mostly-whale-driven playerbase.

## Community Sentiment Summary

**Common themes across all versions and eras:**
1. Players who invested in previous versions feel burned by successive shutdowns and inability to transfer progress
2. Appreciation for the core gameplay loop (deck building, lane combat) tempered by monetization frustration
3. Awareness that this is a recurring relaunch business model
4. Nostalgic returning players from Rise of Mythos / Kings and Legends era are the primary Kings Call audience
5. Active player count is small but stable (200–500 concurrent Steam players in 2024–2025)
6. High-spending "whale" players dominate competitive content
7. Developer responsiveness is described as poor; bugs and balance issues often ignored despite GM/admin acknowledgment

**Representative community quotes (Steam, 2024):**
- "The community is mostly dead or whales, and all other versions of this game died at some point, with this version likely to follow soon."
- "Rise of Mythos was an old MMO strategy game from Gamefuse, and thanks to [community], it's back – in two forms."
- "[Kings Call] is an exact copy of the original it was based off of, minus the localization and years of power creep that the original suffered from."
